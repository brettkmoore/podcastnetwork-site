The Pentatype
Communication Assessment

The Pentatype is a psychometrically validated communication style assessment built on the Five-Dimension Model. It measures five dimensions: Connection, Structure, Conviction, Discovery, and Meaning. Your Core determines your primary communication dimension, the way you think internally, while your Signal shapes how you express yourself to others, creating one of 25 distinct profiles.

Grounded in six phases of psychometric validation (N=4,500+), including confirmatory factor analysis and normative calibration, the Pentatype identifies your natural Psychological Frequency as a host or guest, and maps it to the audience that resonates with your signal.

Take the 20-question assessment and discover Your Core for free. Unlock your complete profile, sub-type analysis, audience psychographics, and strategic recommendations for $149.

What is The Podcast Network?

The Podcast Network is the world's premier podcast ecosystem offering three tiers: a free community with tools, guides, and the Pentatype communication assessment; a done-for-you podcast tour with ghost-written book publication; and an invitation-only elite circle with private jet studio sessions and access to world-class hosts and guests.

Is it free to join?

Yes. The community tier is completely free and includes access to the full podcast community on Skool, automation guides, downloadable tools, and a preview of the Pentatype communication assessment. Premium tiers are available for those seeking done-for-you services or elite access.

What is the Pentatype communication assessment?

The Pentatype is a psychometrically validated communication style assessment built on the Five-Dimension Model. It measures five dimensions: Connection, Structure, Conviction, Discovery, and Meaning. Take the free 20-question assessment to discover Your Core, then unlock your complete 25-type profile for $149.

How does the podcast tour work?

The Tour tier provides a fully managed podcast guest placement service. We match you with vetted shows aligned to your expertise, handle all booking and scheduling, and compile your best episodes into a ghost-written book. The result: authority, reach, and a published work built from conversations that already resonated.

What is The Circle?

The Circle is the invitation-only elite tier. Members gain access to a private jet podcast studio, recording sessions with world-class hosts and guests, in-person events worldwide, and a level of media access and relationships that does not exist anywhere else.

Who is this for?

The Podcast Network serves entrepreneurs, executives, thought leaders, authors, and anyone who understands that podcasting is the most powerful personal media channel in the world. Whether you host a show, want to guest on shows, or both, there is a tier built for your level of ambition.
