The Pentatype
Communication Assessment

The Pentatype is a psychometrically validated communication style assessment built on the Five-Dimension Model. It measures five dimensions: Connection, Structure, Conviction, Discovery, and Meaning. Your Core determines your primary communication dimension, the way you think internally, while your Signal shapes how you express yourself to others, creating one of 25 distinct profiles.

Grounded in six phases of psychometric validation (N=4,500+), including confirmatory factor analysis and normative calibration, the Pentatype identifies your natural Psychological Frequency as a host or guest, and maps it to the audience that resonates with your signal.

Take the 20-question assessment and discover Your Core for free. Unlock your complete profile, sub-type analysis, audience psychographics, and strategic recommendations for $149.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

What is The Podcast Network?

The Podcast Network is a podcast ecosystem that connects hosts with vetted guests, books you on targeted shows, and turns your interviews into a published book. It includes a free community on Skool with tools and guides, the Pentatype Communication Assessment, and a done-for-you program called The Pre-Sold Author that handles podcast booking, ghostwriting, editing, publishing, and launch strategy.

Is The Podcast Network free to join?

Yes. The community is completely free and includes access to other podcast hosts and guests, automation guides, downloadable tools, and the Pentatype Communication Assessment preview. The Pre-Sold Author done-for-you program is a separate paid service for those who want a fully managed podcast tour and published book.

What is the Pentatype Communication Assessment?

The Pentatype is a psychometrically validated communication style assessment that measures five dimensions: Connection, Structure, Conviction, Discovery, and Meaning. It identifies your Core communication type from a 25-type matrix and maps it to the audiences that resonate with your signal. The free assessment takes four minutes. The full profile with sub-type analysis and audience psychographics costs $149.

How do I get booked on podcasts?

The Podcast Network matches you with shows that fit your expertise, audience, and goals. For free community members, we provide guest request boards and host-guest matching. For Pre-Sold Author members, we personally broker 15 podcast bookings through our network, handle all pitching and scheduling, and turn the interviews into a published book. Learn more in our For Guests section or our complete guide on How to Get Booked on Podcasts.

How does The Pre-Sold Author program work?

The Pre-Sold Author is a 20-week done-for-you program. We book you on 15 targeted podcasts, set up a pre-order page before your first interview airs, ghostwrite your book from the interview transcripts, handle professional editing and cover design, and publish the finished book. You walk away with a published book, a built-in audience, and pre-order revenue. Founding member pricing starts at $10,000.

How do podcast hosts find guests for their show?

The Podcast Network provides guest discovery through our community matching boards, where vetted guests are matched to your niche. Hosts also get access to editing and post-production support, automation tools for scheduling and social distribution, and sponsor matching. The community is free to join for hosts at any stage. Learn more about hosting or read our guide on How to Find Podcast Guests.

Can I turn my podcast episodes into a book?

Yes. The Pre-Sold Author program is specifically designed to turn podcast interviews into a professionally published book. We transcribe your episodes, identify what resonated with audiences, and ghostwrite the manuscript in your voice. The book is professionally edited, designed, and published through a distribution channel that puts it on shelves and online retailers. Learn more about our book publishing process.

Who is The Podcast Network for?

The Podcast Network serves entrepreneurs, coaches, consultants, speakers, authors, and thought leaders who use podcasting to build authority and grow their business. Whether you host a show, want to guest on shows, or want to turn your expertise into a published book, there is a path built for your goals.
