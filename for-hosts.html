Frequently Asked Questions

How do podcast hosts find guests for their show?

The Podcast Network provides guest discovery through community matching boards where vetted guests are automatically matched to your niche based on your show's topic, audience, and content style. Browse available guests, review their profiles and communication style from the Pentatype assessment, and connect directly through the platform to book interviews. The community is free to join.

Is The Podcast Network free for hosts?

Yes, the community is completely free to join for podcast hosts at any stage. You get access to the guest matching board, other hosts for collaboration and support, step-by-step automation guides, downloadable templates and tools, and a preview of the Pentatype Communication Assessment. Standard and VIP membership tiers offer additional editing and production support for hosts who want done-for-you services.

What kind of guests can I find on The Podcast Network?

Our network includes entrepreneurs, coaches, consultants, speakers, authors, subject matter experts, and thought leaders across every industry and niche. Each guest has completed their profile and the Pentatype Communication Assessment, so you know their communication style, expertise, audience reach, and whether they align with your show before you book them.

Do you help with editing and production?

Yes. Standard and VIP members get access to editing and post-production support, including episode editing, finding and cutting social clips, audio polishing, chapter markers, and distribution optimization. We handle the technical production work so you can focus on hosting great conversations and building your audience.

What is the Pentatype Assessment?

The Pentatype Communication Assessment is a psychometrically validated assessment that reveals your natural communication style across five dimensions: Connection, Structure, Conviction, Discovery, and Meaning. It identifies your Core communication type and Signal, creating one of 25 distinct profiles. Understanding your communication style helps you attract guests and audiences that align with how you naturally show up. The free preview takes about 4 minutes; the full profile with sub-type analysis and audience psychographics is $149.
