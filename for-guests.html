What is the Pentatype Assessment?

A psychometrically validated communication style assessment built on the Five-Dimension Model. It measures five dimensions: Connection (how you build relationships), Structure (how you organize information), Conviction (how you express opinions), Discovery (how you explore ideas), and Meaning (how you find purpose). Your Core determines your primary communication dimension and how you naturally think, while your Signal shapes how you express yourself to others. The assessment identifies your Psychological Frequency as a podcast guest and maps it to the audiences that resonate with you. The free assessment takes four minutes.
